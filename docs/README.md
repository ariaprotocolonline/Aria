# ARIA Protocol

**Autonomous Real World Asset Intelligence on Mantle.**

ARIA is a non-custodial yield management protocol that puts your WETH and USDC to work across DeFi liquidity pools on Mantle. An AI agent monitors the market every 5 minutes, finds better yield opportunities, and reallocates your capital automatically. Every decision passes through code-enforced safety gates before any transaction is submitted.

You set the risk profile. ARIA handles the rest.

---

## Core Principles

**Non-custodial.** Your vault is deployed as a smart contract owned exclusively by your wallet address. ARIA never holds your funds. You can withdraw at any time, even while the agent is active.

**Per-user isolation.** Every connected wallet gets its own dedicated vault. Your capital is never pooled with other users. Your risk profile, your fee settings, and your allocation decisions are completely independent.

**AI decisions with code-enforced safety.** Claude powers the yield strategy. Before any reallocation executes on-chain, it must pass a series of deterministic safety gates written in code. The AI advises. The contract enforces.

**Full transparency.** Every reallocation is an on-chain transaction with a public hash. Every move ARIA makes is verifiable on the Mantle explorer.
